Hiring standards

  • Integrity: honesty with time, duties, and communication
  • Reliability: consistent attendance and punctuality
  • Safety: follows instructions, uses equipment correctly
  • Respect: professional behavior with team and customers
  • Readiness: ability to work in outdoor/physical conditions (role dependent)

We may request a short call, references, or a quick on-site visit depending on the role.

Hiring process

  1. Apply online (1–3 minutes)
  2. Screening (quick phone call or email)
  3. Interview (virtual or in-person)
  4. Offer (role details + start date)
